```xml
<mxCell id="box1"
  value="Label Text"
  style="rounded=1;whiteSpace=wrap;html=1;fontFamily=Noto Sans JP;fontSize=18;"
  vertex="1"
  parent="1">
  <mxGeometry x="100" y="100" width="120" height="60" as="geometry" />
</mxCell>
```

- `vertex="1"` marks it as a shape
- `parent="1"` attaches to root cell
- `value` is the displayed label (supports HTML when `html=1`)
- `style` is semicolon-delimited key=value pairs
